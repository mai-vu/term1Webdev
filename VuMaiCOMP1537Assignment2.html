Microwave corn on the cob

What you need:

  • Microwave
  • Corn with husk
  • Knife

Now that you have what you need, let's take a look at the how to, step by step

  1. Put the ear of corn in the microwave (with husk on)
  2. Microwave on high for 4 mintutes
  3. Remove carefully
  4. Cut off the bottom of the corn
  5. Slip off the husk and silk
  6. Enjoy! (optional: with butter)
